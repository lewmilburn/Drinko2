Landing 2026

Multiplayer!

You can now play Drinko!3 with friends around the world by linking your games together, kinda like how Kahoot works! By linking your games you'll all be able to see the same screen, and unlock exciting new minigames.

You can still play Drinko!3 in big-screen mode, like how Drinko! and Drinko!2 worked if you'd prefer, but you'll be unable to play some of our newer games.

130 new questions

Drinko!3 brings 130 new questions, truths, dares, and minigames on top of everything from Drinko!2

New Minigames

When playing in our new multiplayer mode, Drinko!3 adds even more minigames that you can play from afar!

Help make Drinko3 the best Drinko ever!

If you have any ideas for questions, truths, dares, minigames, or anything else please email drinko-ideas@pixelset.dev, we'd love to hear from you!

BETA testing!

If you'd like to help us test Drinko!3, please email drinko-testing@pixelset.dev, we'll be opening beta testing in a few months and would love to have you join us!
